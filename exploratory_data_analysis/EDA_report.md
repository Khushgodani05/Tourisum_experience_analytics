# Tourism Analytics Report
**52,922 Transactions | 33,530 Tourists | 30 Attractions | 2013-2022**

---

## Key Findings

### Geographic Distribution
- **Top 5 Countries**: Australia (25.2%), UK (12.7%), USA (11.8%), Indonesia (9.2%), Singapore (5.3%)
- **Top 10 = 79% of all transactions** - highly concentrated market
- **Continents**: Asia (29.7%), Australia/Oceania (28.1%), Europe (25.1%), Americas (15.5%), Africa (1.7%)

### Temporal Trends
- **Peak Period**: 2015-2017 (58.5% of transactions)
- **2016 Peak**: 12,818 transactions (24.2%)
- **COVID Impact**: 2020-2022 only 1.5% (98% collapse)
- **Seasonality**: July-September peak months

### Customer Satisfaction
- **79.16% high satisfaction** (4-5 stars)
- **6.24% dissatisfaction** (1-2 stars)
- **14.61% neutral** (3 stars)

### Attraction Performance
- **Sacred Monkey Forest**: 24.9% (dominant)
- **Top 10 attractions = 85.6%** (extreme concentration)
- **Only 30 of 1,698 attractions** receive transactions
- **Preferred Types**: Nature (25%), Beaches (20.6%), Religious (12.7%)

### Destination Concentration
- **Douala**: 85.3% (high risk)
- **Cameroon**: 86.9%
- **Need diversification**

---

## Critical Issues

1. **Extreme Concentration Risk**
   - 85.6% in top 10 attractions
   - 85.3% in single city (Douala)
   - 24.9% in one attraction

2. **COVID Recovery Challenge**
   - 98% decline from peak
   - Need to rebuild to 2016 levels

3. **Underutilized Segments**
   - Business travel only 1.2%
   - 1,668 attractions untapped
   - Africa market only 1.7%

---

## Strategic Priorities

### 1. Diversification (Critical)
- **Reduce top 10 dependency**: 85.6% → <70%
- **Reduce Douala concentration**: 85.3% → <75%
- **Add 5-10 new attractions** annually
- **Develop N'Djamena & South Region**

### 2. Market Strategy
- **Protect (60% budget)**: Australia, UK, USA
- **Develop (25% budget)**: India, Malaysia, Canada
- **Explore (15% budget)**: Africa, E. Europe, S. America

### 3. Seasonal Optimization
- **Peak (Jul-Sep)**: +15-25% pricing, advance booking
- **Off-peak**: 20-30% discounts, local focus, events

### 4. Segment Growth
- **Business**: 1.2% → 5% (corporate packages, facilities)
- **Couples**: Romantic experiences, premium packages
- **Family**: Educational programs, kid amenities

### 5. Experience Enhancement
- **Maintain**: 79% satisfaction via quality audits
- **Fix**: 6% dissatisfaction via 24hr response
- **Upgrade**: 15% neutral via value-adds


**Strengths**: Strong markets (Australia, UK, USA), high satisfaction (79%), clear seasonality
**Risks**: Extreme concentration (one attraction 25%, one city 85%)
**Opportunity**: 1,668 untapped attractions, business travel 5x growth, emerging markets

**Strategy**: Diversify portfolio, optimize seasons, grow segments, maintain quality
